Welcome to the World of Westworld

About the Series

"Westworld" is a dystopian science fiction television series that explores the complexities of artificial intelligence, human desires, and morality. Created by Jonathan Nolan and Lisa Joy, it debuted in 2016 and is based on the 1973 film of the same name.

The Storyline

The series is set in a futuristic amusement park where human-like robots called "hosts" cater to the guests' every desire, often leading to unpredictable consequences.

Why Westworld Stands Out

The show combines philosophical depth, thrilling action, and a stellar cast to create an unforgettable experience.

Terminology

Guest
